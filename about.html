About Mashtato

Mashtato was founded with a simple mission: to serve the most delicious loaded potato bowls in Terengganu. Our passion for quality ingredients and amazing flavors drives everything we do.

We started as a small business catering to students at Politeknik Besut Terengganu, and we're proud to continue serving this community with our signature dish.

Each bowl is carefully prepared with fresh, high-quality ingredients to ensure you get the best dining experience possible.
